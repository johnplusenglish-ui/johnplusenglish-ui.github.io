TaskWrite an essay discussing two ways individuals can make a difference to environmental issues (avoid plastic / raise awareness / choose how to travel). Explain which is more effective and give reasons. 220–260 words.
Flagged Corrected

Amid ongoing social and technological change, few issues are as relevant as environmental one. Our planet getting hotter and hotter is a threat to future lives, and only our habits can put the brakes on it.

Many government policies have tried to push people to travel in alternative ways: to give just one illustration, the introduction of restricted zones. Although this may reduce the pollution, banning cars altogether is unviable, as most people rely on their car to get to work.

A more consequential issue emerges: the gigantic plastic consumption. Its presence damages all ecosystems, being them underwater or above ground. Task 4 · Work & Technology Your class has attended a panel discussion on how technology is changing the world of work. Write an essay discussing two of the ways technology is affecting work. Explain which change you think is more significant, giving reasons. 220–260 words.

How is technology changing the world of work?
• remote working  ·  • automation of jobs  ·  • work-life balance

"Working from home means people never really switch off."
"Machines will eventually replace most of the jobs people do today."
"Technology gives workers the freedom to choose when and where they work."
📌 Pointers
Choose two pointsRemote working and automation make the strongest pair — both have clear arguments for and against.
Your thesisAutomation is more significant because it is irreversible, whereas remote working is a choice.
Body paragraph 1Remote working: flexibility and productivity gains, but risk of isolation and blurred boundaries.
Body paragraph 2Automation: efficiency for employers, but job displacement and skills gaps. Your stronger point.
Useful vocabularydisplacement, redundancy, upskilling, flexibility, irreversible, to render obsolete, to disrupt
Watch outCompare the two points directly: "While remote working affects how we work, automation threatens whether we work at all."

Tips for Viola — C1 Advanced Essay Writing

📐
Keep a clear four-paragraph structureIntroduction (thesis) → Body 1 (point 1) → Body 2 (point 2 + comparison) → Conclusion. Each body paragraph should have one clear focus.
🎯
State your thesis clearly — and argue it throughoutYour introductions are confident. Make sure the argument promised in the intro is resolved in the body, not just restated in the conclusion.
"This essay argues that, while X cannot be dismissed, Y is ultimately the more significant factor..."
⚖️
Compare your two points explicitlyDon't just describe both — weigh them against each other.
"While changing travel habits requires systemic infrastructure, reducing plastic use demands only small, immediate individual changes."
🔗
Use precise academic linking language
❌ "at the end of the day" → ✅ "ultimately"
❌ "What they are forgetting is that, however" → ✅ "However, what is frequently overlooked is that..."
🧠
Watch for Italian interference
circolare → "get around" · declinazione → "form / type" · fare la spesa → "do the shopping" · investire su → "invest in"
🔢
Use they/their for generic singular referenceReferring to a teenager or individual generically? Use they/their — not his. This error appears in all three essays.
✅
What you're already doing wellStrong introductions, genuine C1 vocabulary range (ascribed to, prevails, consequential, misbehaviour), and effective complex sentences. Focus now on tighter paragraph logic and consistent register.
